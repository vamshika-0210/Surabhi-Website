Facilities and events at surabhi gir goshala

Our Goshala complex: includes

  • Parama Karuna Sri Nitai Gouranga Temple (50–60 people)
  • Party Hall (75–100 people)
  • Kitchen (serves up to 200 meals)
  • Open Dining Hall (up to 200 guests)
  • Washrooms and dressing facilities

We host & allow bookings for Sattvic Events: including

  • Birthday Celebrations
  • Engagement Ceremonies
  • Naming Ceremonies
  • Upanayana
  • Marriage & Reception
  • Spiritual Gatherings or Retreats
Visiting hours: 8:00 AM – 6:30 PM
