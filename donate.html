The Three-Point Seva Programme

Our activities are centered around a Triangle of Seva, with Gomaata Seva as the first and foremost point, supported by Annadana Seva and Yugadharma (Hari Nama) Seva.

1. GOMAATA SEVA

Serving, protecting, and nurturing Desi Gir cows with love and devotion. We began with just two cows and today care for 15, aiming to grow to 50 cows in the next few years. Every cow is treated with affection, proper shelter, healthy food, and medical care.

3. YUGADHARMA / HARI NAMA SEVA

Chanting, Bhajans, and Sankirtan form the heart of spiritual upliftment. Daily, our devotees chant the Hare Krishna Maha Mantra, conduct Bhajans, Satsangs, and Nama Sankirtan — filling the atmosphere with divine energy.

Visitors are welcome to join and participate in all these spiritual activities at the Goshala.

We also accept contributions: in-kind

  • Cow fodder (green, dry, or feed)
  • Grains, vegetables, ghee, jaggery, oil
  • Kitchen groceries for Annadana
  • Volunteering for Goshala maintenance and temple seva
