Mission

Compassion in daily action

We rescue, nourish, and rehabilitate cows with dignity, guided by the belief that seva blossoms through mindful routines.

  • Round-the-clock caretakers ensuring gentle handling.
  • Nutrition plans balancing fodder, minerals, and herbs.
  • Community engagement that transforms kindness into habit.
Vision

Sanctuary that sustains

We imagine a goshala where every cow thrives, volunteers feel at home, and ecological cycles stay in harmony with tradition.

  • Expand shaded pasture and rainwater harvesting swales.
  • Offer immersive goseva experiences for families and schools.
  • Model a replicable goshala blueprint for allied communities.
