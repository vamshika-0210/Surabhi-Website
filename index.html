Welcome to Surabhi Gir Goshala, a divine place where love, service, and spirituality come together. We are devoted to protecting and serving Desi Gir cows, feeding the needy with pure sattvic meals, and spreading the holy vibration of the Lord’s names through Sankirtan and Bhajans. Every visitor is welcome to participate in our seva and experience peace and divine happiness.

Kamadhenu Shrine

The heart of daily seva

Around the sanctum, caretakers gather for morning aarti and gentle grooming rituals that anchor our herd in calm devotion.
