About Surabhi

Care that keeps every gentle soul thriving.

Surabhi Goshala is a sanctuary built on seva, sustainable routines, and heartfelt community participation. Every dawn begins with aarti, healing herbs, and hands that believe kindness is an everyday practice.

120+ Cows sheltered with dignity
42 Weekend volunteers on rotation
18 Acres of shaded grazing & fodder
Our Mission

Compassion in daily action

We exist to rescue, nourish, and rehabilitate cows with dignity. Every routine — from fodder prep to bedtime checks — is designed to keep our herd calm, healthy, and loved.

  • Holistic care: Balanced nutrition, native herbs, and regular veterinary wellness.
  • Community seva: Guided volunteer slots for families, schools, and corporate teams.
  • Regenerative campus: Rainwater harvesting, composting, and fodder grown onsite.
Our Values

Principles that anchor Surabhi

  • Compassion: Every life is sacred; our schedule bends to their comfort.
  • Integrity: Transparent operations, audited books, and open visitor hours.
  • Stewardship: Thoughtful use of resources and mindful expansion of the goshala.
  • Learning: Workshops that spread goseva practices across allied communities.
Daily rhythm

From sunrise bells to moonlit check-ins

A typical day begins with mangala aarti, warm water sponge baths, and fresh-cut fodder. Afternoons are for physiotherapy walks, shade rotations, and hoof care. Nights end with soothing chants and final patrols.

Fodder Kitchen

Hydroponic fodder, herbal supplements, and jaggery mixes portioned for each cow.

Healing Wing

Isolation bays with heat lamps, herbal wraps, and a dedicated vet-on-call desk.

Community Courtyard

Story circles, go puja, and seva workshops that connect visitors with our herd.
