About Our Goshala

We exist to protect and care for cows — with compassion, dignity, and community support.

Our Mission

To provide safe shelter, nutrition, and medical care for cows in need, while promoting kindness and sustainable living within our community.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every life deserves care and respect.
  • Integrity: Transparent and responsible stewardship of resources.
  • Community: Bringing people together for a meaningful cause.

Team & Volunteers

A small, dedicated team supported by a growing volunteer network. Join us for weekend seva, feeding, cleaning, and outreach.
